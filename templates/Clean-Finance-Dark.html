Workflow

From zero to production in four steps.

1

Connect

Paste a live landing-page URL. ResetUI captures copy, media, links, hierarchy, and brand signals.

2

Describe

Tell ResetUI what visual direction you want, or start from high-fidelity templates and prompts.

3

Refine

Review the generated page in a visual canvas. Tweak sections, branch ideas, or extend with chat.

4

Deploy

Export standalone HTML when ready. Keep versions for future iteration and handoff.
