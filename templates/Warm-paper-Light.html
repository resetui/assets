I Workflow / The Premise
Count 04 · Pages 008–012

[ Premise ]

ResetUI treats your existing website as context—not a layout to clone and not content to throw away.

Context, not guesswork.
Redesign, not a clone.
Output you can ship.

ResetUI begins with a real landing page. It captures the copy, calls to action, links, media, hierarchy, colors, fonts, logo references, and visual signals that make the source useful.

Your design prompt supplies the new direction. The result is intentionally redesigned, streamed section by section, editable through chat, tracked through versions, and exportable as usable front-end code.
