§ 02 THE REPORT / WHAT RESETUI IS 1,240 WORDS · 4 MIN READ

Not another blank canvas. A newsroom for redesigns — where every URL becomes context, every prompt becomes direction, and every section can be edited.

ResetUI's premise is simple: a landing-page redesign should begin with the page that already exists. The source URL supplies copy, hierarchy, links, media, colors, fonts, and brand signals before a single new section is generated.

The workflow borrows from editorial production. Capture is the brief, generation is the draft, chat edits are revisions, version history is the archive, and export is the final issue ready for handoff.

Under the surface, ResetUI coordinates website capture, brand extraction, streaming generation, project-aware chat updates, canvas controls, and deterministic HTML assembly into one repeatable product flow.

The category is crowded with prompt-only generators. ResetUI instead treats the existing page as product memory, giving builders a redesign that still understands the original offer, navigation, calls to action, and media.

"Prompt-only tools gave us pretty guesses," said Daniel Park, a builder using ResetUI. "Starting from the live page gave the redesign a reason to exist."
