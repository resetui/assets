[ 005 ] / Product Principles

Five
promises
by design.

The constraints that keep AI-assisted redesign grounded, editable, and genuinely useful after generation ends.

01

Start with the real page.

The website URL is essential context. ResetUI captures existing copy, calls to action, media, links, structure, and brand signals before redesign begins.

02

Redesign, do not clone.

The source supplies intent; your prompt supplies the shift. The result preserves what matters without tracing the screenshot or repeating the old layout.

03

Stream the work.

Planned skeletons appear immediately and finished sections arrive as they are generated, so progress stays visible instead of hiding behind a blank wait state.

04

Keep every iteration.

Chat edits, undo, redo, and restores commit recoverable design versions. You can explore a direction without losing the version that worked.

05

Finish with usable code.

The final committed design exports as a clean standalone HTML/CSS document: a real front-end deliverable, not a screenshot trapped in a preview.
