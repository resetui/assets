The ResetUI method

The best redesigns do not erase what already works. They preserve the copy, calls to action, imagery, brand signals, and section intent that make a page yours—then rebuild the visual experience around a clearer direction. ResetUI keeps that source context present from first capture to final export.

Interactive workflow

01

Capture the source

Paste a public website URL. ResetUI captures the page structure, copy, links, calls to action, imagery, typography, colors, and brand context.

02

Define the direction

Describe how the page should look and feel. Your prompt supplies the new visual direction while the captured site supplies the content and intent.

03

Generate and refine

Stream a redesigned page into the canvas section by section, refine it through project-aware chat, restore versions, and export the final code.
